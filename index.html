INSTRUCTIONS



CONTROLS

Use WASD to move around.
Use the mouse to look around.
Use V to toggle view mode.
Use X to shoot cannonballs.
Use SpaceBar to pause the game.
Use I to show instructions.

POINTS

The aim is to collect all treasures.
Earn points by collecting chests (5 points) and treasures (10 points).
Earn kill points by drowning the pirate ships (20 points).
Getting hit by cannonballs will cost you health.
You lose the game if your health reaches 0.
You win the game if you collect all treasures.

Happy Treasure Hunting! ×
